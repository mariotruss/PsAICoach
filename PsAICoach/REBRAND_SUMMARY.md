## New Name: PsAICoSys
**Pronunciation**: "Sai-Co-Sis" (like "Psychosis" but with AI emphasized)

**Meaning**: 
- **PsAI** = Psychosis + AI
- **Co** = Companion/Cognitive
- **Sys** = System

This name clearly positions the app as an **AI system specifically designed for psychosis and schizophrenia**.

## Branding Changes

### Positioning
- **Before**: General mental health companion
- **After**: Specialized AI support for psychosis, schizophrenia, and psychotic experiences

### Messaging
- **Focus**: Reality validation, AR-assisted grounding, psychosis-specific support
- **Target Users**: People experiencing psychosis, schizophrenia, or dissociative symptoms
- **Key Differentiator**: AR reality checking specifically designed for validating perceptions

### Updated Locations
1. **Landing Page** (`web/landing.html`)
   - Title: "PsAICoSys - AI Support for Psychosis & Schizophrenia"
   - Hero: Emphasizes psychosis management

   - AR section: Specifically for distinguishing real vs unreal perceptions

2. **Main App** (`web/index.html`)

3. **App Logic** (`web/app.js`)
   - Welcome message mentions psychosis support

   - AR overlay shows "PsAICoSys Reality Check"

### Design Implications

The rebranding makes the app's purpose immediately clear:
- **Medical focus**: Psychosis, not general anxiety/depression
- **Therapeutic tool**: AR for reality testing
- **Specialized support**: Features designed for this specific condition

### Disclaimers Updated
